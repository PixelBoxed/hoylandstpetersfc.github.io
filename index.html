Interested in playing?

Choose your numbers

Select 8 numbers between 1 and 59 to make up a line. You can choose as many lines as you like for a donation of Â£1 per line, per week.

Register your lines

Fill in our online form to tell us about your chosen lines and we'll arrange to send you out a welcome email containing next steps.

Read your welcome email

In it we'll everything you need to know to get your lines in the game. If we're in the middle of an active game, we'll send you a follow up message when we get a winner and the next game starts.

Set up your payments

Using a Standing Order, send us the right amount weekly to cover your lines. We ask for weekly so that you can cancel and remove your lines from the game at anytime. There's no long-term commitment.

Check your results

Every week we'll use the numbers from the National Lottery Lotto draw to check off the numbers in your line. Check off all 8 and you're a winner!

Claim your prize

The prize pot varies depending on how many players are in the game and how many lines they each have, but we'll confirm the amount and BACS transfer it to you.
