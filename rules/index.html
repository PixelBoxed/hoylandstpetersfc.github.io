--- layout: page back: false title: "Game rules" ---
  1. The lotto bingo is open to any person who wants to support {{ site.name }}
  2. Each person will pay £1.00 per week, per line that they are playing
  3. Choose any 8 different numbers from 1-59 inclusive. These numbers will remain unchanged until such time when it has been won and a new game starts.
  4. From the National Lottery Lotto draw on a Saturday Night, any number that matches a number in your lines will be marked off until somebody has completed all 8 numbers.
  5. The bonus ball number will not be included, only the 6 main lotto balls.
  6. From the money collected 50% will go to the prize fund and 50% will go to {{ site.name }}.
  7. Money raised will go mostly into the age groups raising it.
  8. Any winning person in arrears of 3 weeks or more will not be paid out and the fund will roll over to the next pot.
  9. Where there are joint winners in this situation the prize fund will be split between those winners who are not in arrears.
  10. The {{ site.name }} Committee’s decision is final.
